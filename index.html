Nayana Phukan, the author of the novel “Dawn in Florence” is a software engineer and an avid traveler. Nayana, who has been working as an IT professional for the last nine years, started her writing journey as a travel blogger and eventually developed an interest in writing fiction. She currently lives in Mumbai, the city of dreams and believes in enjoying every moment of life.
